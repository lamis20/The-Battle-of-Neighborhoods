Capstone Project: The-Battle-of-Neighborhoods

City of Toronto Neighbourhoods comparative study

 

Introduction

As COVID-19 hits everywhere in the world, I decided to take Toronto as a case study, the study will characterize Toronto neighbourhoods by features include people testing positive for COVID-19, health units, populations, income, education, housing and labour.

The purpose of this project aims to create an analysis of features for the City of Toronto as a comparative analysis between neighborhoods to show the effective of COVID-19 on each neighborhood. According to the Government of Toronto report on June 5th, 2020 the number of cases are 11,835 with 883 deaths.
